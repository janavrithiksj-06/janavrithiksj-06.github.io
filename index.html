Text Analysis of Online Banking Reviews

Master's Thesis @ TIAS School for Business and Society
Jun 2025 - Aug 2025
Python (NLTK) TF-IDF Sentiment Analysis Regression (OLS)

Challenge

Identify the key service and emotional factors influencing customer satisfaction in digital banking using large-scale online reviews.

Approach

  • Collected and analyzed 21,956 reviews from Trustpilot via Kaggle.
  • Implemented a pipeline with TF-IDF feature extraction and lexicon-based analysis (NRC Emotion Lexicon).
  • Built OLS regression models to quantify the impact of specific emotions (Joy, Trust, Anger) on star ratings.
Key Result
Emotional sentiment factors explained ~49.7% of customer satisfaction variance, proving emotions predict churn better than service keywords alone.
