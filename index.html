Master's Thesis: Text Analytics of Online Banking Reviews

TIAS School for Business and Society

Jun. 2025 - Aug. 2025

Python NLP Sentiment Analysis TF-IDF Regression

Challenge

Understand key drivers of customer satisfaction in online banking to help financial institutions prioritize service improvements and enhance customer retention.

Approach

  • Collected and analyzed 21,000+ customer reviews across multiple banking platforms
  • Implemented NLP pipeline: tokenization, lemmatization, and TF-IDF feature extraction using NLTK and scikit-learn
  • Applied sentiment analysis using VADER and advanced emotion lexicons (NRC) to detect nuanced customer sentiments

Results & Impact

Identified 5 primary service drivers (security, user experience, support, fees, innovation) explaining 68% of satisfaction variance, enabling targeted improvement roadmap.
  • Delivered actionable insights that help banks focus engineering and service redesign efforts on high-impact areas
  • Demonstrated advanced NLP and statistical modeling skills applicable to customer feedback analysis in financial services
